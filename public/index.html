How to Start Trading on PSX
1

Get a National Identity Card (NIC)

You need a valid Pakistani NIC or NICOP (for overseas Pakistanis). This is the base requirement for all investment accounts in Pakistan.

2

Open a CDC Account (Investor Account)

Register with the Central Depository Company (CDC) via any NCCPL member broker. Your shares are held here electronically. Go to cdcpakistan.com ↗ to learn more.

3

Choose a Licensed Broker

Select a SECP-licensed broker. Popular choices include:

JS Securities Arif Habib Ltd AKD Securities IGI Securities Topline Securities Foundation Securities
4

Fund Your Trading Account

Transfer funds via online banking to your broker's account. Minimum capital varies by broker — most allow starting with as low as PKR 10,000–25,000.

5

Place Your First Order

Log into your broker's platform or app. Search a stock by its symbol (e.g., ENGRO, LUCK, HBL). Enter quantity and price, then submit. Orders execute during market hours (9:15 AM – 3:30 PM PKT).

6

Settlement (T+2)

PSX uses T+2 settlement — your shares and funds settle 2 business days after the trade. Until then, the transaction is pending. Always keep sufficient margin in your account.

What is Forex Trading?

🌍 The World's Largest Market

The foreign exchange (forex) market trades over $7 trillion per day — dwarfing all stock markets combined. It operates 24 hours a day, 5 days a week across global financial centres.

📊 How Currency Pairs Work

Currencies are quoted in pairs like EUR/USD = 1.08, meaning 1 Euro buys 1.08 US Dollars. The first currency is the "base" and the second is the "quote." You profit when the exchange rate moves in your predicted direction.

💡 Major, Minor & Exotic Pairs

Majors (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY) have the highest liquidity and tightest spreads. Minors don't include USD. Exotic pairs like USD/PKR have wider spreads but can present opportunities for Pakistani traders.

⚠️ Leverage Risk Warning

Forex brokers offer high leverage (50:1 to 500:1). While this amplifies profits, it equally amplifies losses. Most retail forex traders lose money. Always use stop-losses and never trade with money you can't afford to lose.

📐 R:R Quick Check

What is R:R?

Risk:Reward ratio compares how much you risk vs how much you stand to gain. A 1:2 R:R means risking $100 to make $200. Only take trades with R:R of at least 1:1.5 or better.

✅ 1:2 — Risk $100, gain $200
✅ 1:3 — Risk $100, gain $300
❌ 1:0.5 — Risk $100, gain $50

💡 How to Invest in US & UK Stocks from Pakistan
1
Choose a Broker: Use international brokers like Interactive Brokers, TD Ameritrade, or local options like EasyInvest. Check if they're available for Pakistani residents.
2
Open an Account: You'll need your CNIC, proof of income, and a bank account. Account opening takes 1-5 business days.
3
Fund via Remittance: Transfer USD from Pakistan using banking channels. SBP allows individuals to remit up to $100,000/year for investment purposes.
4
US Tax (W-8BEN): Non-US residents fill a W-8BEN form (15% withholding tax on dividends). Capital gains are generally not taxed by the US for non-residents.
5
FBR Reporting: You must declare foreign assets/income in your Pakistani tax return. Failure to report is a legal risk.
